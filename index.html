About Ian

Building a
body of work.

I'm a researcher, writer, speaker and founder working at the intersection of technology, AI, democracy, policy and culture. Over more than a decade, I have built initiatives reaching hundreds of thousands of people, shaped policy frameworks, and contributed to global conversations on AI governance.


Currently a Visiting Fellow at the London School of Economics and a Chevening Scholar completing an MA in AI Ethics and Society at Birkbeck, University of London. I believe the most consequential questions about AI are not technical — they are political, democratic and moral.
